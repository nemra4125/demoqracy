Let them eat barcodes!

demoQRacy is a tool that makes offline voting easier using mobile phones.

  1. Create a set of things to be voted on. Paintings, science fair projects, cookies, anything!

  2. We make it simple to print one barcode (specifically, a QR Code) for each item in your set.

  3. Display your new QR codes and tell people to scan them with their phones. One scan = one vote!
